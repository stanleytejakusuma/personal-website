June 17, 2022 Ekta raised $60M from Global Emerging Market

Ekta → Bali blockchain company with an L1 ecosystem focused on bridging real-world asset on-chain

Global Emerging Market → $3.4B alternative investment group focusing on emerging markets

Reason: Ekta early stage of building blockchain-powered prod (i.e. NFT marketplace, hybrid CEX, GameFi, real estate investment business). Ekta’s L1 is already fully operational.

Usage: Development of Ekta ecosystem, liquidity for NFT marketplace & hybrid CEX, development of MetaTrees (Plant-to-Earn game), marketing, tech dev team, and partnerships

### Ekta

- Ecosystem

1.  Ekta Portal: physical device that rewards crypto acting as an endpoint node. Users receive unique NFT keys with exclusive whitelist access to all of Ekta’s ecosystem. When all of the portal is employed, users are rewarded 1:1 with the price of Ekta.

2.  NFT Marketplace

3.  MetaTrees: Plant-to-Earn gaming focusing on passive income while initiating real world change through tokenized trees and verified carbon units.

4.  Ekta Island: Real world estate located off the coast of Bali (Gili Gede). MetaTrees and future events would be hosted here.

5.  Ekta Assets: fractional NFT of real world assets (i.e. pre-vetted luxury assets, premium real estate, yacht, private jet, etc) with the opportunity to earn passive income through profit sharing.

- Blockchain

1.  Mainnet: EktaChain

2.  Bridge from ETH to EktaChain

3.  Staking follows a period of start and close date Gwhich users could lock in capital for 60 days. APR 18%.
