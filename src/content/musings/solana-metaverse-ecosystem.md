Genre: J-RPG, Turn-based, “Pokemon”

Capital Required: Free-to-play

Launched on: Open Beta November/December 2021

- A free-to-play set in the world of Antik where players are going to battle with monsters then rewarded with the defeated monsters as NFTs.

- Provides a platform for players that realizes the monetary value of their time spent on playing video games and DeFi enthusiasts through yield farming and staking.

- Gaming mechanisms:

1.  The world of Antik is filled with relics, artifacts, and monsters called Nefties.

2.  A player could have a squad filled with 5 Nefties.

3.  Each battle is a turned-based tactical J-RPG style.

4.  Gamemodes:

    1.  Soloplayer → Receive NFT of Nefties that are defeated + AURY each completed chapter

    2.  Multiplayer → Requires AURY staking. Rewards based on weekly and monthly PvP leaderboards

5.  Annually held tournament:

    1.  Golden ticket that could be farmed from the game as entry.

    2.  Rewards would be in the form of significant cash prizes, rare items, and the permission to imprint designs on future Nefties.

    3.  Defeated Nefties would be lost forever.

**Star Atlas**
==============

Genre: Space Exploration, Factions, Spaceships, Hybrid

Capital Required: 115 ATLAS (~ $21 USD) Late November 2021, Cost varies based on career options.

Launched on: October 2021 (with following updates)

- Set in the year 2060, the universe is controlled by three major factions: MUDI Territory – humans, ONI Region – alien races, and Ustur Sector – sentient androids. You have the ability to influence the outcome of this intergalactic resource, territorial, and domination struggle.

- The video game’s visuals are built upon <u>Unreal Engine 5</u>’s nanite technology that enables cinematic quality in real time.

- Star Atlas has a system that encourages claim staking to expand the gameplay. To this end, Star Atlas enables players to captain spaceships to scan and discover celestial/terrestrial assets. After discovery, rich claims that are staked can be mined, refined, and traded on the network.

- This strategy provides opportunities for career options: trading raw and refined ore, cargo hauling, crafting retail components, etc.

- Gameplay:

1.  Main goal of the game is to claim mining sites, which the resource would be used as career and income opportunities.

2.  The game is divided into three zones: Faction-secured safe zone (new players start here), Medium-tiered zone (secured by mercenary groups where combat is not allowed but assets will be reset if destroyed), and Wild Frontier zone (full combat and destruction of assets is permanent).

3.  All visible stars are real assets that could be visited. There will be no skybox or limit since exploration is done visually.

4.  Combat is unique, players could indicate their stance as aggressive or passive to signal desired combat engagements. Weapons have different types that have respective benefits and drawbacks.

5.  The resource obtained in the game could be marketed both in the Star Atlas marketplace itself or outside.

6.  Another feature is character customization.

- Currently, the team is in development of creating an AI in-game and a new race.

- Players could stake ATLAS in safe zones as a node to generate passive income.

- DeFi implementations in Star Atlas, including Lending, AMM, Yield Farming.

# **Solchick**

Genre: Role-playing

Capital Required: 3 to 5 SOL

Launched on: October 2021 (with following updates)

- A play-to-earn game, SolChick is a fantasy multiplayer battle system similar to Divinity using third-person perspective

- Gameplay:

  - Solchick utilizes NFTs as the characters, meaning that the players would play as chickens that have several abilities depending on their class.

  - There are two game modes: PvP in the form of MOBA style and Raids (PvE) in which players team up to defeat bosses.

  - Bond and Training systems are methods of nurturing the player’s chicken to level up and be stronger.

  - For each of the 10,000 limited SolChick, players could customize attributes that the chicken wears. These attributes have rarity which would provide multiple benefits.

  - Players not only customize the attributes that the SolChick wear, but also primary (Stamina, Health, Dexterity, and Intelligence) and secondary stats (Movement Speed, Focus, Haste, Spell Crit Chance, etc)

  - On reaching level 45 and level 60, players would receive a quest to pick a class and mastery of that class respectively.

- Rewards:

1.  SolCoins to power up their SolChicks and purchase in-game items.

2.  Shards of Love ($SLC) from weekly pet happiness levels and winning battles which could be used to breed and sell SolChicks.

3.  Tomes of Power ($SPower) and Tomes of Relearning ($SLearn) through battle and reselling them in marketplace

4.  Chicks ($CHICKS) which have governance rights through weekly leaderboard/MMR ratings.

5.  Breeding SolChicks and selling it at the marketplace.

- Though the initial 10,000 supplies of SolChicks could pose a problem, the developers allow the NFT owners to stake their SolChick by letting others borrow the NFT, which would allow unlimited SolChick to be created through breeding.

# **Project SEED**

Genre: ARPG (Currently), MMORPG and Battle Royale (Soon)

Capital Required: Free-to-play

Launched on: December 2021

- Project SEED is a metaverse ecosystem that develops mobile blockchain gaming through multi-chain and cross-platform hybrid technology.

- The ecosystem includes Game HUB, GameFi (NFT, DeFi, and Marketplace), DAO, ESports, and Growth program (incubation for limited budget projects).

- To support the system, Project SEED uses a proof-of-play mechanism where users can play games and have the opportunity to earn rewards. More on https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/3327960.3332386

- The project is currently developing Outland Odyssey – an Action Role-Playing Game (ARPG), click to move and third person view, similar to diablo style.

- Blockchain Integration and Functionality:

  - Multi-chain wallet : hold tokens in different blockchains

  - $SHILL & Game tokens : in-game currency and receive benefits

  - Vault : DeFi products (Staking, Chain Bridge, etc)

  - SEEDex : in-game DEX

  - NFT : user’s assets

  - Marketplace : on-chain trading assets

- Outland Odyssey Gameplay:

  - Built on Unity game engine in iOS/Android platform

  - Several key features: NFT Rental Marketplace (in-game assets), Dungeon Creator (from players who stake $SHILL), NFT Creator Program (earn royalty from created NFTs)

  - The background story revolves in a land called Avoria, where the players try to save the metaverse on the brink of extinction. To this end, players can hunt, capture, and train creatures called Zeds while also uncovering the truth about the Keeper of Existence (mystical beings that can deliver the world from extinction).

  - Combat will be fast-paced and focused. Players could dodge and strike in real-time.

# **Genopet**

Genre: MMORPG

Capital Required: Free-to-Play

Launched on: Demo Online

- Genopet is designed to be a mobile game that combines the evolution of *Tamagochi*, training and battling of *Pokemon*, and the crafting of *Animal Crossing*. Genopet’s core concept is built on the incentive from the wearable industry. The gameplay mainly converts players real-life movement into XP to progress in-game, in doing so players could join the in-game economy to earn rewards, hence Move-to-earn.

- Gameplay:

  - Similar to *Pokemon GO*.

  - The game relies on wearables (i.e. FitBit and Strava) technology with the notion of real-life movement incentives.

  - Players could battle each other in a turned-based battle arena, allowing for an asynchronous fighting system.

  - Genopet’s gameplay include several elements such as Genopets (NFTs that evolves based on the player’s personality), XP (metadata that increases accordingly with grinding actions), Refined Crystals (Power ups that can be crafted using native tokens), and Habitats (place where Genopets live, evolve, and train).

  - Players could sell or rent their Genopets and Habitats to other players in the ecosystem to earn native tokens.

  - Main goal of the game is to move around in the player’s daily life, in doing so the player would earn XP, which would be used to improve the Genopet.

- The project provides an incentive for both Free-to-Play and Pay-to-Play players:

  - Free-to-Play : F2P players would have the ability to only mint their own Genopets, walk in real life to earn XP, then sell their leveled Genopets.

  - Pay-to-Play : Players who buy a habitat are considered a P2P. Using the habitat, the Genopets could be trained more effectively and augmented using a Refined Crystal – increasing its value.

- As of late November 2021, there is no public release of the game yet, however players could test the demo on Solana Devnet ([<u>https://demo.genopets.me/</u>](https://demo.genopets.me/)).

- Native Tokens:

  - $GENE : Utility and Governance token. Holding GENE tokens gives priority access when an in-game content launches. GENE is also used in refining a crystal and augmenting a Genopet to a certain element.

  - $KI : Reward token. KI is earned through the player's real-life movement and battling other players through the battle arena. It is also used in refining and Genopet augmenting.
