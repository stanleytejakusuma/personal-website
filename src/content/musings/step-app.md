Project started as a fair launch – no VC rounds before the public sale.

On the intersection of the fitness ($100B) and GameFi ($200) industry,

Step is the **first** protocol to be built on $FITFI – blockchain focusing on the M2E industry.

In simple terms, Nike Run club but with rewards!

At launch, Step was hit with a major bottleneck, $GMT $GENE. Solving this issue, Step made the protocol’s SDK open source for devs to build on the Step Metaverse.

Into the app itself, Step is creating a huge metaverse to walk, jog, run, socialize, play, and earn. It is the embodiment of physical experiences in the metaverse – the peak of crypto adoption. 👀

Step-nomics

- Half of the market bought tokens are distributed to stakers

- 10d cooldown on locking tokens with added benefits

- Staked LP tokens get 50% more reward than usual!

- Team will always buy back using half of the play income

- Here, governance may vote to either burn or distribute those tokens

$FITFI

Usage: Governance

Distribution: Total Supply (5M) Mining/M2E (30%) Staking Liquidity (20%) Team (15%) Marketing (15%) Public Sale (14%) Partners (6%)

M2E Income

- Micro-tx: non-SNEAK purchase (map styles, skins, etc)

- Tournament: Compete step count. Winners get unique skins and a dedicated leaderboard. (requires ticket; Step takes 5% all accrued ticket fees)

NFT Income

- Marketplace: 2.5% fee on all trades

- Sales: Limited edition sneakers issued once/month
